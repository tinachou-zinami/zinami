About Us

Premier Medical
Device Distributor

Founded in 2020, Zinami Biotechnology Co., Ltd. is a premier medical device distributor in Taiwan. We specialize in value-added product platforms, dedicated to empowering surgeons with the most suitable and advanced clinical solutions.

Our core team comprises industry veterans from world-leading medical device companies, bringing decades of clinical expertise to the table. This deep-rooted experience translates into an extensive and robust network across Taiwan's surgical specialties.

As an independent distributor, Zinami has the agility to carefully curate our global partnerships — ensuring we introduce only the most innovative and highest-quality medical technologies to the Taiwanese market at competitive values.
